Research Projects

  • High-quality plasmonic nanostructures for detection and modulation of new types of excitons, e.g., dark excitons, localized excitons, interlayer excitons, biexcitons in 2D TMDCs or van der Waals heterostructures.
  • Construction of nanophotonic prototype devices, such as nano-light source, nanoantenna, tunable nanolasers, smart sensors, and other photonic-circuit elements.
  • Plasmonic control of valley emissions in 2D TMDCs.
